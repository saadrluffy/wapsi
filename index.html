Wapsi — an Urdu word meaning return. Return to what matters. Return to each other. Return to a world worth living in.

Every stitch is a statement. Every garment, a call to come back.

We are a women's fashion brand born from the belief that clothing can be art, and art can change minds. Each piece in our collection carries a visual message — against racism, for humanity, for the earth — because we believe what we wear is what we stand for.

Fashion is one of the most powerful platforms for expression in the world. We use that platform to speak — for the silenced, for the overlooked, for the planet. Wapsi is not just clothing. It is a movement stitched into fabric.

Founded with love, worn with purpose.
